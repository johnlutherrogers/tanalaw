Highly Experienced Attorney in
International Business and Corporate Matters

John Rogers has counseled corporations and individuals for three decades in a wide variety of business and professional settings. His experiences cover business operations in more than 10 different jurisdictions world-wide and he is keenly aware of the nuances and importance of cross-cultural matters on legal strategy.

As a former in-house General Counsel , Rogers is adept at spotting and dealing with critical issues. He speaks frequently to attorneys and legal organizations about business entitles, corporate governance and business ethics and teaches US law, primarily to Chinese attorneys.

An area of particular interest is Rogers ‘ assistance to Chinese law firms and companies in vetting, engaging and managing law firms and other professional service firms in the US and around the world. This expertise, gained as an in-house legal counsel, provides a significant cost savings to client while ensuring superior performance.
